Bird of The Day

Eurasian Owl (Bubo bubo)

Also known as the Uhu, is one of the largest owl species in the world, with females reaching a wingspan of over 6 feet! These feathered giants have impressive hearing thanks to their distinctive ear tufts, which help them pinpoint prey in the darkness.

Latest Updates

Labrador duck, Camptorhynchus labradorius

The Labrador Duck, sadly extinct, was a mystery even in its time. This unique duck had a strangely shaped bill and was once found along North American coasts.

Pink-necked green pigeon, Treron vernans

This beautiful pigeon from Southeast Asia has a green body with a yellow breast and a blue crown. It's a popular sight in the rainforest canopy.

Southern cassowary, Casuarius casuarius

This flightless bird from New Guinea and Australia is the closest living relative to the dinosaur velociraptor. Watch out for its sharp claws!

Secretarybird, Sagittarius serpentarius

This African bird is a tall, long-legged predator with a crest of feathers on its head. It's known for stomping its prey like a secretary with a giant feather duster!
